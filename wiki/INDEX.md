# Agent Factory Wiki -- Index

> The knowledge base for building production-quality AI agents.

## How to Use This Wiki

This wiki is the structured reference layer for the Agent Factory: meta-skills and tooling can retrieve articles here to ground answers in shared vocabulary, patterns, and research summaries. Read it alongside the **23-module course** (`../course/README.md`): modules map to concept articles, while research notes deepen specific topics. Start from a concept when you are designing or reviewing an agent; use research articles for comparisons, citations, and architecture debates; use examples as concrete anchors for what to emulate or avoid.
